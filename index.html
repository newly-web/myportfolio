Comming Soon: Neighborhood Watch

I’m looking forward to launching Neighborhood Watch and seeing the positive impact it can have on building safer, more connected communities.

This full-stack web app allows users to post and report the disappearance of loved ones, whether they’re pets or people, within their neighborhood. The goal is to make it easier for neighbors to share information and work together during critical situations.
